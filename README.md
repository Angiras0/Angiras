We are building an IoT-enabled smart helmet platform that transforms passive safety gear into an intelligent, connected protection system.
Our core product includes:
A Smart Helmet, and
A Modular Attachment System that can convert any compatible conventional helmet into a smart helmet.
The goal is simple: move from reactive protection to proactive safety.
For civilians — including riders, delivery workers, and site workers — the system provides:
Automatic accident detection
Automated SOS triggering
Live location sharing with emergency contacts
Heart rate monitoring
Self-charging capability
For defense applications, we extend the platform with advanced capabilities such as:
Object detection assistance
Night vision and thermal sensing
Rear-view camera integration
Dedicated internal display
End-to-end encrypted communication
Radiation-absorbing materials for enhanced protection
Our vision is to build a modular intelligent safety platform that serves both civilian and defense sectors — upgrading helmets from simple protective gear into life-saving, technology-driven systems.
We are not just building a product.
We are building the future of intelligent protective wearables
