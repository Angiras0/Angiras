About ANGIRAS

Engineering
Intelligent Safety
Systems


ANGIRAS is a deep-tech safety startup building the next generation of intelligent wearable systems. Our mission is to transform ordinary helmets and safety equipment into life-saving intelligent devices using IoT, embedded sensors, and real-time communication.

Our modular architecture means riders and workers don't need to replace their existing gear — our technology snaps onto any standard helmet, converting it into a connected smart device instantly.

From motorcyclists navigating highways to soldiers in the field, from miners underground to emergency responders — ANGIRAS is building safety infrastructure for humans in dangerous environments.

Founder Atharv Angiras Dubey

01
Product 01 — Smart Helmet
Smart Helmet
Modular Intelligent Protection

A full-face helmet integrated with IoT sensors, AI-powered accident detection, GPS, and emergency alert systems. Our modular design fits any standard helmet — no full replacement needed.

  • Crash detection & emergency SOS alert
  • Real-time GPS tracking
  • Built-in speaker & microphone array
  • LED status display — Intercom / Music / GPS
  • Universal helmet mounting system
MVP — Active Development
02
Product 02 — Smart Intercom
Beak Box
Intelligent Rider Intercom

The Beak Box is ANGIRAS's proprietary smart intercom unit — shaped like an eagle's beak for aerodynamic profile. Attaches universally to any helmet and enables crystal-clear rider-to-rider mesh communication.

  • Central control hub: volume, track skip & power
  • Integrated microphone & speaker system
  • Universal helmet mount — tool-free install
  • Mesh network — multi-rider group comms
  • Noise cancellation up to highway speeds
MVP — Active Development
Core Technology

Built on
Deep-Tech
Infrastructure


Every ANGIRAS product is engineered from first principles — combining embedded electronics, wireless mesh networks, real-time sensor fusion, and AI decision systems into a compact, helmet-mountable module.

📡
IoT Connectivity

Always-on mesh networking enables real-time data transfer between riders, devices, and emergency services.

🧠
Embedded AI

On-device machine learning for crash detection, hazard prediction, and rider behavior analysis — no cloud latency.

⚡
Sensor Fusion

IMU, gyroscope, accelerometer, and temperature sensors combined for rich real-time situational awareness.

🔊
HD Audio DSP

Digital signal processing delivers crystal-clear communication even at 120kmph with full noise cancellation.

🛡️
Modular Design

Clip-and-play modular hardware compatible with any standard helmet. No replacement. No waste.

🌐
Safety Alerts

Instant SOS broadcasting to emergency contacts, cloud, and nearby ANGIRAS units on crash detection.
